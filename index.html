What is Asther Wallet?

It is a decentralized wallet that can be executed on different networks, such as Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, etc.

This wallet allows you to securely and reliably store and manage your digital assets without relying on intermediaries such as banks or other financial institutions.

Our priority is decentralization in accordance with blockchain principles.

• Non-custodial blockchain wallet • Integration for NFTs • Send and receive funds • Backup • Exchange

Enjoy!
